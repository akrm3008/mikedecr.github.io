Michael DeCrescenzo

Ph.D. Candidate, Political Science

University of Wisconsin–Madison

I am a political science Ph.D. student at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, where I am also an affiliate of the Elections Research Center and an Honored Instructor.

My work develops and applies new Bayesian approaches to the study of U.S. elections, election administration, public opinion, and causal inference. You can find my academic CV here.

I expect to finish my Ph.D. and begin a non-academic career in 2020.
